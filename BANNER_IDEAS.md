# 🎨 Cool Things You Can Do with Your GitHub Profile Banner

This guide provides ideas and tips for creating an eye-catching GitHub profile banner!

## 🌟 What We Created

We've created a professional banner for your **Customer Sales Insights** project featuring:
- **Gradient Background**: Beautiful purple-to-pink gradient that reflects the beauty industry theme
- **Project Title**: Bold, prominent title with shadow effects for depth
- **Subtitle**: Clear description of your project focus (Beauty Trends in Atlanta, GA)
- **Tech Stack Badges**: Visual representation of technologies used (Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Jupyter, Beauty Data)
- **Data Visualizations**: Mini bar chart and line chart to showcase the analytical nature of the project
- **Decorative Elements**: Floating circles representing data points/bubbles
- **Professional Typography**: Clean, readable fonts with proper hierarchy

## 🎯 Banner Specifications

- **Dimensions**: 1280x640 pixels (2:1 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: PNG with transparency support
- **File Size**: ~96KB (optimized for fast loading)
- **Location**: `images/github_banner.png`

## 📝 Best Practices

1. **Keep it Simple**: Don't overcrowd the banner
2. **High Contrast**: Ensure text is readable against background
3. **Consistent Branding**: Match colors with your profile
4. **Optimize File Size**: Keep under 200KB for fast loading
5. **Mobile-Friendly**: Test how it looks on different devices
6. **Update Regularly**: Keep content fresh and relevant
7. **Tell Your Story**: Let your banner reflect your journey
